Nanybot - Refund Policy

Refund Policy

At Nanybot, we stand behind our AI-powered content creation services with a straightforward and customer-friendly refund policy. We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all our services, including our article writing platform.

Our 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you can request a full refund within 30 days of the purchase date. You are not required to provide any specific reason for the refund, though we welcome your feedback to help us improve our services.

What's Covered

  • All subscription plans for article.nanybot.com
  • One-time credit purchases
  • Add-on services and features

Refund Process

To initiate a refund:

  1. Log into your Nanybot account
  2. Visit the billing section
  3. Click on "Request Refund" button
  4. Fill out a brief refund request form

Alternatively, you can contact our support team at support@nanybot.com for assistance with the refund process.

Refund Timeline

Once approved, refunds are processed within 3-5 business days. The time it takes for the refund to appear in your account depends on your payment method:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-10 business days
  • PayPal: 2-3 business days
  • Bank Transfers: 5-7 business days

Enterprise Customers

For enterprise customers with custom contracts, refund terms may vary based on the specific agreement. Please refer to your contract or contact your account manager for details.

Exceptions

While we aim to make our refund process as straightforward as possible, there are a few exceptions:

  • Refunds cannot be issued for content that has already been generated and downloaded
  • Promotional credits or bonus credits are non-refundable
  • Accounts found to be in violation of our terms of service are not eligible for refunds

Feedback

While not mandatory, we appreciate feedback from customers requesting refunds. Your input helps us improve our services and better serve our community. When submitting a refund request, you'll have the option to share your experience and suggestions.
